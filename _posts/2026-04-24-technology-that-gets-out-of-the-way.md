I have been working enough as an indenpendent consultant to realize that the biggest
asset I have is my critical thinking. People pay me to assses what they currently do 
and based on what I have seen working in other places could apply to them.

This means that until some company pays me big money to be a forward deploy engineer and
its on my interest shape your organization to work with my product. I will always be 
skeptical to companies offering.

This week Anthropic released Claude Design. The idea is to provide an interface to visualize
the result of your prompts towards a specific design task. Of course, with every new feature
there's the whole cult of doomers dog-feeding us the theory everything will be automatized.

For me this triggers a way more interesting angle. Before, if I needed to create an online
identity. I would probably stick to the basics (website, domain, email, etc) but now, because
those are so easy to solve...I have extra energy to go deeper: define a brand identity, create
a design system...

Those were things I had 0 clue that existed, but make so much sense! I have only been able to
explore those concepts because the most rudimentary aspects of the process are now easier to perform.
Now this is where it gets interesting...in the same way that wave of vibecoded apps released into
the wild are now collapsing... my first attempts of building a brand identity suck!
But now, I can go to a real professional and say...hey you are a creative person, let's skip the
part where you build a crappy Wordpress website for peanuts and help me to build this identity.
Let's explore together as human beings, why I'm doing this, why it matters so much, and help me
channel your creativity into materializing this.

That ladies and gentleman is the only AI-want in my life (sorry I had to). Get me out of the fucking
machine and allow to work on the moving things. I want technology that gets out of the way and automates
all the terribly boring stuff. Let me focus on what moves me and people value.
